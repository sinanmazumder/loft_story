FROM OUR OWN BOOK

We are a few friends who got together to create stories with words, pictures, film, poetry, music… A small, vibrant team that works out of a sun-drenched loft, surrounded by musical instruments, cameras, canvas and paint. We all walked in from different, yet closely connected worlds: advertising, film, creative writing, fine art… Together, we have spent over 50 years in our respective fields. We’ve worked with some of the world’s most respected organisations and brands from diverse arenas: healthcare, tourism, fashion, infrastructure, education, retail, finance & banking, social… We’ve created stories that have touched hearts as well as bottom-lines. You, too, must drop by sometime for a Chai, a story and some music!

SUCCESS STORIES

A Novell handbook

When Novell wanted a Managers’ Handbook that would excite and engage the user, we brought to life a new Bible: A Gospel of Guidelines. This compact paperback emulated the Bibles of yore in look and feel, and caught the users’ imagination so much, it went into its second edition. And yes, managers’ awareness about processes and practices improved multi-fold.

Moving stories

some BRANDS we touch

some CHARACTERS

Pramod started his career photographing weddings and developing black-and-white images. After 25 years in advertising, writing copy and directing campaigns, he caught up with his old muse – poetry. Today, Pramod is a performing storyteller, author and coach. Yes, he also takes black-and-white pictures of very colourful stuff, and has a 3-member band called The Rickshaw Muse.

Zac is the quietest of us all. His Zen-like poise and deeply resonant moods help us add meaning to every story. With his legendary way with people and verse, Zac chairs our monthly poetry event, called – you guessed it – Poetry With Zac. Now in his 4th year, Zac loves listening to Jack Johnson when he’s not chasing cats.

Shikhin is a footballer, automobile enthusiast and art director. He creates vibrant stories for diary products, jewellery, fashion, FMCG and organic food; his work for the traffic police created quite a flutter in the media. Got a long weekend? Ask Shikhin for a ride towards the Ghats, with a few stories to spare.
